Built a Refractor Telescope from scratch. The whole telescope body was designed in SOLIDWORKS and 3D printed, except for the tube itself, the lens, and the eyepiece.

Designed to be modular, where various sections of the telescope can be screwed in or out. For example, there are two focuser designs that both screw into the telescope: A screw focuser for higher precision, and a more standard rack and pinion design. A DSLR Camera adapter can also be attached.
